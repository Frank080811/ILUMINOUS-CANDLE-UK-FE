About Nelux Candles

Where Scent Meets Soul — Handcrafted with Heart

At Nelux, every candle tells a story — a harmony of fragrance, craftsmanship, and emotion. Hand-poured in small batches, our candles transform any space into a sanctuary of calm and connection.

Pure Soya Wax

Eco-conscious, biodegradable, and clean-burning for a longer, toxin-free glow.

Artisan Longevity

Enjoy up to 72 hours of burn time, with a fragrance that lingers beautifully.

Safe by Design

Lead-free wicks, non-toxic oils, and durable glass jars — crafted for peace of mind.

✨ Hand-Poured with Passion

Each Nelux candle is poured by hand to ensure unparalleled quality and care. Inspired by nature, memory, and mood, our fragrances are made to evoke emotion — not just scent.
